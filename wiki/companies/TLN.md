# Talen Energy (TLN)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** both — but note the stock is near its 52-wk high (low margin of safety)

> **Why a page now (2026-06-20):** TLN is the highest-beta pure-play in the nuclear-for-AI cluster — owner of Susquehanna and the Amazon data-center co-location deal that helped kick off the entire theme. Cluster B power priority.

## One-line thesis
TLN owns the **Susquehanna nuclear plant** with an **Amazon AWS data-center campus co-located behind the meter**, plus a gas fleet. **Which means** it is the purest, highest-torque public bet on hyperscalers contracting nuclear baseload directly — but with smaller scale and more beta than [[CEG]]/[[VST]], and now trading near its highs.

## Role in AI / datacenter power
- **Susquehanna nuclear plant** (~2.5 GW) in PJM — large, dispatchable carbon-free baseload.
- **Amazon AWS co-location** — data center campus drawing power behind the meter; the deal that arguably started the nuclear-for-AI co-location trend.
- Gas fleet provides peaking / round-the-clock complement.
- Smaller, more leveraged balance sheet = higher torque to power prices and new PPAs.

## Bull case
- **Pure-play torque** — fewer moving parts than CEG; a single new hyperscaler deal or favorable PJM ruling moves the stock hard.
- **+55% revenue growth** and fwd P/E ~18 (cheapest of the nuclear trio on forward earnings).
- **Amazon co-location** validates behind-the-meter nuclear, a model others want to copy.
- **PJM capacity prices** have risen sharply — direct tailwind to merchant nuclear economics.
- Active **buyback / capital return** story; management has been shareholder-friendly.

## Bear case / risks
- **Near 52-wk high (-3%)** — little margin of safety; the easy re-rating has happened.
- **Behind-the-meter regulatory risk** — FERC scrutiny of co-location arrangements (the ISA dispute) could cap the model's scalability.
- **Small fleet / single-asset concentration** — Susquehanna is the crown jewel; an outage or relicensing issue is material.
- **High beta** — smaller cap, more leverage = bigger drawdowns in any AI-power scare ([[ai-bubble-debate]]).
- **Merchant power price exposure** — earnings swing with PJM prices.
- **Valuation re-rate already paid** vs the de-rated [[CEG]].

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **FERC / PJM rulings** on behind-the-meter co-location — the key swing factor for the model.
2. **New hyperscaler PPAs** beyond Amazon.
3. **Capacity auction prices** in PJM.
4. **Capital return pace** (buybacks).
5. **Relicensing / uprate** of Susquehanna.
