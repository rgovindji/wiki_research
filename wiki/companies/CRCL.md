# Circle Internet Group (CRCL)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** low · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
The dominant *regulated* US-stablecoin first-mover (issuer of **USDC**) — but its core revenue is *reserve yield it keeps*, and that economic model is now under simultaneous attack from regulators (yield-ban drafts) and a 140-firm **Open USD** consortium that shares the yield with adopters and includes Circle's own biggest distributor, Coinbase.

## Bull case
- **USDC is the #2 stablecoin** with deep liquidity, exchange/DeFi integrations, and cross-chain transfer (CCTP) — real network effects that don't vanish overnight.
- **Regulated / compliant first-mover** under the GENIUS Act framework — trust + transparency vs. Tether; the "institution-safe" stablecoin.
- **Stablecoin TAM is large and growing** — if total stablecoin volume compounds, USDC can grow in absolute terms even as share is contested (a rising-tide counter).
- **Down ~52%** — a lot of bad news may be in the price if the Open USD launch slips or underwhelms.

## Bear case / risks
- **Open USD consortium (NEW 2026-06-30, [[2026-06-30-open-usd-consortium-vs-circle]]).** 140+ firms (Visa, Mastercard, Stripe, BlackRock, BNY, Coinbase, Ripple, Google, Shopify) launching a multi-chain rival (OUSD) that **shares reserve yield with adopting businesses** — attacking Circle's core revenue line — with the **distribution rails Circle lacks**.
- **Coinbase defection.** Coinbase (Circle's biggest distribution partner, ~50% of USDC reserve revenue) is *in* the consortium — the single sharpest signal against CRCL.
- **Revenue ~entirely reserve interest** → rate-sensitive AND the exact economics the consortium undercuts; a **regulatory ban on stablecoin yield** (March-2026 CLARITY draft, −18% reaction) compounds it.
- **Broken chart** — below all EMAs, ~52% off highs; no trend support.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Open USD** — does it actually launch (date/specifics), and do the named partners (esp. Coinbase) shift volume off USDC?
2. **USDC circulation** trend — the cleanest fundamental tell.
3. **Stablecoin-yield regulation** — outcome of the Fed rules / CLARITY drafts.
4. **Reserve-revenue split** — any change to the Circle–Coinbase economics.
