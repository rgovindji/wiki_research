# Onto Innovation (ONTO)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
Pure-play process-control / metrology + advanced-packaging inspection vendor levered directly to the HBM and 2.5D/3D packaging ramp that AI accelerators depend on — high-quality niche, but the stock sits ~4% off its high at a ~155x trailing / ~41x forward multiple with revenue stalled flat (+0.5% YoY), so the multiple is pricing a recovery that hasn't shown up in the numbers yet.

## Bull case
- **Advanced-packaging inspection leader** — directly levered to HBM stacking and CoWoS / 2.5D-3D heterogeneous integration, the AI-accelerator bottleneck.
- **Metrology / process control** is a structurally growing, high-margin slice of semicap as nodes shrink and packaging complexity explodes.
- **Lithography + film metrology** breadth gives multiple AI-capex attach points.
- **Clean balance sheet**, niche moat, takeover-attractive scale.

## Bear case / risks
- **Revenue is flat (+0.5% YoY)** despite the AI narrative — the forward multiple (~41x) prices an acceleration not yet visible.
- **Trailing ~155x P/E** — extreme; any guidance miss de-rates hard.
- **Small and concentrated** — lumpy memory/packaging capex cycles drive quarter-to-quarter volatility.
- **Customer concentration** in a handful of leading-edge memory/foundry buyers.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Advanced-packaging / HBM bookings inflection — the whole thesis.
2. Memory-capex recovery timing (Samsung/SK Hynix/Micron).
3. Gross-margin trend as mix shifts to higher-value tools.
4. Forward revenue guide vs. the multiple's embedded expectations.

## Verdict
Neutral / medium conviction. **Book-fit: watchlist.** Right exposure (HBM/packaging), wrong entry — flat revenue against a ~41x forward / ~155x trailing multiple near highs. Wait for a bookings inflection or a de-rate.
