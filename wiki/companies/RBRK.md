# Rubrik (RBRK)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
High-growth (+45-48% rev) data-security / cyber-resilience SaaS — backup, ransomware recovery, and increasingly "data security for the AI era" (governing what data AI agents can touch) — that has de-rated ~30% off its high; the growth and AI-security narrative are strong, but it's still GAAP-unprofitable and priced on revenue, so this is a higher-beta growth bet, not a safe compounder.

## Bull case
- **45-48% revenue growth** with improving losses — durable land-and-expand in cyber-resilience.
- **AI-era data-security angle** — as enterprises deploy AI agents, governing/securing what data those agents access is a fast-emerging budget line; Rubrik is positioning here.
- **Ransomware-recovery is non-discretionary spend** — cyber budgets are resilient even in downturns.
- **De-rated ~30%** — better entry than at the highs for a high-growth name.

## Bear case / risks
- **GAAP-unprofitable** with -$289M net income; path to durable profitability is the open question.
- **Revenue-multiple valuation** — ~10x sales; compresses fast if growth decelerates.
- **Crowded backup/data-security market** — Cohesity (private), Veeam, Commvault, and platform vendors all compete.
- **AI-security is early** — more pitch than proven revenue line today.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Net-new ARR / NRR trend — is the land-and-expand engine still accelerating?
2. Path to GAAP profitability and FCF.
3. Concrete traction on the AI-data-governance product.
4. Competitive displacement (wins vs. Cohesity/Commvault).

## Verdict
Bull / medium conviction. **Book-fit: watchlist → small core-add candidate** for the AI-software-security sleeve. Strong growth + topical AI-security narrative + ~30% de-rate, but unprofitable and richly priced on sales — size accordingly.
