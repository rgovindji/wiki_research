# Western Digital (WDC)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term with cyclical awareness

## One-line thesis
The HDD-focused remainder after the [[SNDK|SanDisk]] spin-off — AI "data exhaust" drives nearline HDD demand for cold-tier storage, with margins on a path from 30%s to 50%+ and a clean balance sheet (debt-free post-spin).

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **Nearline / capacity HDDs for cold-tier AI data storage** — hyperscalers need petabytes of cheap storage for training data lakes
- Not contracted to [[NVDA]]; rides the same hyperscaler capex wave
- Storage tier of AI infra alongside [[MU]] (HBM) and [[SNDK]] (NAND/SSDs)

## Bull case
- **Path to >50% gross margins near term**, potentially 60% by late 2028 (analyst views)
- **Debt-free** following monetization of the SanDisk stake
- **HDD oligopoly** with Seagate (only two large suppliers at scale) → pricing discipline
- **AI data growth** — every byte of training data produced requires storage; nearline HDD economics still beat all-flash for cold storage
- **Stock up ~176% YTD 2026** on the AI storage thesis

## Bear case / risks
- **Cycle-peak P/E paradox applies (NEW).** WDC is +40% in the past month and increasingly being called "undervalued." Storage / memory P/Es look cheap at cycle peaks because the E is at peak. See [[ai-bubble-debate]] for the framework. **Which means:** WDC is a leveraged trade on the AI data-exhaust storage cycle, not a buy-and-hold compounder. The two-supplier HDD oligopoly is real but doesn't immunize WDC from cycle risk.
- **HDD is a sunset technology** — long term, all-flash will erode the moat (the question is *when*, not *if*)
- **Cyclical** — every storage cycle has booms and busts; current demand strength may be more cycle than secular
- **Customer concentration** — large hyperscalers represent a significant share of unit demand
- **Consolidation done** — the "two-supplier" structure is fully priced in already

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Gross margin trajectory** — the entire bull case rests on the 50%+ path
2. **HDD vs. flash mix-shift signals** at hyperscalers
3. **Capacity / capex commentary** — supply discipline holds?
4. **Free cash flow** — debt-free → return-of-capital potential

## Recent developments
- **2026-05-11** — WDC **+40% in past month** on AI memory supercycle confirmation. **Non-GAAP gross margin crossed 50% for the first time** (confirming the bull case's path to 50%+ margins). **20% dividend increase**. Mizuho lifted PT alongside SNDK / MU calls.
