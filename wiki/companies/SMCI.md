# Super Micro Computer (SMCI)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** low · **Time horizon:** tactical

## One-line thesis
Specialty AI server builder with deep Blackwell support and a 4U liquid-cooled HGX B300 portfolio — AI hardware tailwinds remain real, but the **lingering accounting / legal overhang** (Hindenburg short report, Ernst & Young resignation in 2024, ongoing remediation) caps conviction until trust is fully restored.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **AI server OEM** — HGX B300 8-GPU 4U liquid-cooled systems; full Blackwell + Vera CPU portfolio
- **Reference designs** with NVIDIA RTX PRO Blackwell Server Edition GPUs
- Historical leader in dense GPU server racks for AI training

## Bull case
- **Deep Blackwell + Vera support** across multiple form factors
- **Liquid cooling expertise** in DLC-2 capturing 98% of heat
- **Faster product time-to-market** historically vs. Dell/HPE on bleeding-edge configurations
- **Stock has remained volatile** — leveraged exposure to AI capex cycle
- **Hyperscaler edge / RTX PRO** systems expand TAM

## Bear case / risks
- **Accounting / legal overhang** — Hindenburg short, EY resignation, restatement → lingering trust deficit
- **Share shift to Dell/HPE** as enterprise customers prefer governance certainty
- **Margin compression** as AI server market becomes more competitive
- **Customer concentration** — small number of large buyers
- **Inventory + receivables risk** during fast hardware transitions

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Quarterly filings** — clean, on-time, fully reconciled
2. **Auditor stability** — any further changes
3. **Customer wins / losses** vs. Dell, HPE
4. **Operating margin trajectory**
5. **Inventory turns** on Blackwell ramp

## Recent developments
- **2026 YTD** — Stock **−7% YTD 2026** despite April rebound ($22.77 → $27.40). Underperforming [[DELL]] (+67%) and [[HPE]] (+20%) by a wide margin. Per 24/7 Wall St analysis: "investors should watch for whether the legal overhang shows signs of resolution, since that's the gating factor for multiple expansion." Consistent with wiki's neutral / low-conviction stance.
