# STMicroelectronics (STM)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term · **Listing:** NYSE ADR (also Euronext / Borsa Italiana)

## One-line thesis
European mixed-signal / power-electronics leader — part of NVIDIA's **800V DC power architecture** ecosystem for AI factories, but core franchise (auto, industrial) is currently in cyclical downturn limiting near-term operating leverage.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **800V DC power architecture partner** alongside ABB, [[ETN]], Schneider, [[VRT]], Infineon, Navitas
- **Power MOSFETs / SiC / GaN** for data center power conversion
- Smaller relative to Eaton / Vertiv but a core silicon supplier in the chain

## Bull case
- **AI data center power tailwind** as the 800V architecture transitions
- **Auto / industrial recovery** could compound with AI exposure
- **SiC capacity** investments position for EV + data center demand
- **European / non-China supply** geopolitically advantaged

## Bear case / risks
- **Auto cycle weakness** is the dominant near-term headwind
- **Industrial market softness** in Europe
- **AI exposure is a smaller revenue mix** vs. core auto/industrial
- **Competitive intensity** from Infineon, ON, Navitas
- **Currency / geographic mix** complexity

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Auto + industrial trough** — when does it inflect?
2. **AI data center revenue mix** — is it material yet?
3. **SiC capacity utilization**
4. **Operating margin trajectory**
