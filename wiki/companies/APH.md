# Amphenol (APH)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
Diversified interconnect and connector leader — every AI server rack, every fiber run, every power cabinet pulls Amphenol content. A more diversified, less optics-pure way to ride the AI infrastructure build than [[COHR]] / [[LITE]].

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **High-speed connectors** for AI server racks and switches
- **Cable assemblies** for AI fabric
- Listed in industry NVDA supply-chain references alongside Samtec and TE Connectivity
- Indirect — sells to OEMs ([[DELL]], [[SMCI]], [[JBL]]) and hyperscalers, not directly to NVDA

## Bull case
- **Diversified end markets** — AI/cloud, automotive, defense, mobile, industrial — beats pure-play optics on volatility
- **Content per AI rack growing** — denser, faster, more thermally challenging assemblies = more APH content
- **Defense / aerospace** as a non-AI ballast
- **M&A track record** — disciplined acquirer in interconnect

## Bear case / risks
- **Less levered to AI** than pure optical names — lower beta on the way up *and* down
- **Acquisition-driven growth** — execution risk, integration risk
- **Premium multiple** — quality-compounder pricing leaves less margin of safety
- **Some segments cyclical** (mobile, automotive)

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **AI / data communications segment** revenue mix and growth
2. **Operating margin direction** — is content-per-rack flowing through?
3. **Acquisition pace and quality**
4. **Mobile / automotive segment macro sensitivity**
