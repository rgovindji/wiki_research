# Reddit (RDDT)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
Hyper-growth (+70% rev) social platform with two AI-linked engines — a premium data-licensing pipe to LLM trainers (Google, OpenAI) and a fast-scaling ad business benefiting from being a top citation source in AI-search answers — now ~38% off its high after a brutal de-rate, making it the most volatile but most directly "AI-monetizing-content" name in the group.

## Bull case
- **+70% revenue growth** and recently GAAP-profitable — rare combination of scale + acceleration.
- **AI data-licensing** — unique, human-written, opinion-rich corpus that LLM makers pay for (Google, OpenAI deals); a high-margin, AI-native revenue stream peers lack.
- **Ad business inflecting** — under-monetized user base, improving ad stack, international + logged-out expansion runway.
- **AI-search citation moat** — Reddit is one of the most-cited sources in AI answer engines, driving both relevance and traffic.

## Bear case / risks
- **Google-search-traffic dependence** — algorithm changes / AI-overview cannibalization could hit referral traffic and engagement; the single biggest risk.
- **Still richly valued** — ~33x forward, ~50x trailing despite the de-rate; high expectations embedded.
- **Data-licensing is lumpy** — deal renewals are negotiated, not recurring-guaranteed.
- **Engagement/moderation risk** — community governance and user-trust dynamics can swing usage.
- **High volatility** — the ~38% drawdown shows how violently sentiment reprices this name.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Daily-active-user + engagement trend vs. Google-traffic dependence.
2. Data-licensing renewal/expansion cadence (Google/OpenAI and new entrants).
3. Ad ARPU ramp and international monetization.
4. Sustained GAAP profitability and FCF.

## Verdict
Bull / medium conviction. **Book-fit: core-add candidate** for the "AI-content-monetization" sleeve — the cleanest pick-and-shovel on LLMs needing human data + AI-search citations. Highest volatility in the group; the Google-traffic dependence is the falsifier. Size for the beta.
