**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term compounder with AI-monetization optionality

## Role in AI / aerospace
- **AI predictive maintenance**: real-time engine health monitoring → service-cost optimization → margin compounder
- **Digital twin** at engine level → real-time wear prediction → workscope automation
- **AI blade inspection 50% time reduction**: automation in MRO (maintenance, repair, overhaul) drives operating leverage
- **C3 AI + Collins Aerospace collaboration** (broader RTX ecosystem) — adjacent partner; GE not directly involved

## Bull case
- **Post-separation focus**: GE Aerospace is now a pure-play; capex + capital allocation discipline improved vs conglomerate era
- **LEAP engine service margins**: 50/50 JV with Safran; CFM dominant on 737 MAX + A320neo; service revenue compounds
- **Aging fleet drives spares** (parallel to [[HWM]] commercial engine spares +48% YoY): same demand vector
- **AI predictive maintenance $500M-$1B opportunity by 2028** — high-margin software-style economics on top of hardware
- **21.8% Q1 operating margin** — already at software-quality margins on best-in-class aerospace ops
- **787 ramp to 8/month** + 777X EIS 2027 = engine demand pipeline
- **Defense business**: F404 (F/A-18, T-7), F414 (Gripen, KAI T-50), Boeing F-15EX
- **GE Hitachi Nuclear (GEH)** — SMR Xe-100 commitment (via parent), nuclear SMR optionality

## Bear case / risks
- **Cyclical aerospace demand**: 787/777X production missed deliveries multiple times — engine demand follows airframe
- **Customer concentration**: Boeing + Airbus dominate
- **Service revenue lumpy**: depends on flight hours; AI mitigation but not eliminates
- **CFM JV structure with Safran**: any disagreement on capex or pricing splits margins
- **Premium valuation post-AI narrative** — GE has rerated significantly on AI predictive maintenance pitch
- **Hexcel/Howmet supplier dependency**: structural materials (Ti-Al, carbon fiber) constrain GE production rates
- **Russian VSMPO titanium normalization** if Ukraine resolves = supply chain re-pressure
- **AI monetization timeline**: $500M-$1B by 2028 = $200-$300M annually new — not yet visible in segment reporting
