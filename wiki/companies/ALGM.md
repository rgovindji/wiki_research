# Allegro MicroSystems (ALGM)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis

Magnetic position and current sensors attach mechanically to every motor joint a humanoid will ever have — under-appreciated in current humanoid screens because the business is 70%+ auto-exposed today and obscured by the auto cycle, but the per-unit content scales with the actuator count.

## Bull case

- **Magnetic sensor + power IC duopoly** for motion control. Position, speed, current sensor ICs plus motor driver ICs, power management ICs, and isolated gate drivers. The exact stack required to control a humanoid actuator.
- **Per-humanoid socket count.** A general-purpose humanoid has 30–50+ actuated joints. Each joint needs at least one position sensor and usually one current sensor. Per-unit ALGM content is much higher than per-EV content.
- **Q4 FY2026 beat.** Adjusted EPS $0.17 beat consensus. Revenue surpassed expectations.
- **12 analyst Buy ratings, 0 Sell.** Average PT $54.42 (+14.95% upside) with high of $62.
- **Three decades of magnetic-sensing expertise** with an installed automotive-grade IP base. Hard to displace once designed in.
- **Market cap up 58% over the past 12 months** as the auto cycle floor formed and AI / robotics adjacency was repriced.

## Bear case / risks

- **Auto exposure caps near-term torque.** ~70% of revenue is automotive. Auto cycle has been weak; until it recovers, the humanoid attach narrative cannot lift the multiple alone.
- **Negative trailing margins.** Operating margin -2.7%, net margin -10.1% TTM. Profitability dependent on volume recovery.
- **Short interest 10.1% of float.** Notable bearish positioning; 7.5 days to cover.
- **Insider selling over past 90 days** (Form 4 filings) per public reporting. Most likely routine 10b5-1, but worth tracking.
- **Substitute risk.** Magnetic position sensing competes with capacitive and inductive alternatives. ALGM's IP base is strong but not unique on every joint.
- **No named humanoid OEM design wins disclosed yet.** Pure-play exposure remains thesis-driven rather than confirmed.

## Key questions / what to watch

- First public confirmation of a humanoid OEM design win (Tesla, Figure, Apptronik, Agility).
- Auto cycle recovery — EV unit volumes and global light-vehicle production.
- Q1 FY2027 (May 2026 print) gross margin trajectory back toward 50%+.
- Industrial restocking pace — analog semis are positioned to benefit.
