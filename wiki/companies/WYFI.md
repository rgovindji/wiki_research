# WhiteFiber, Inc. (WYFI)

**Stance:** neutral (speculative) · **Conviction:** low · **Time horizon:** high-risk / event-driven · **Verdict:** the *most speculative tier* of the neocloud bucket — a sub-scale, cash-burning AI-HPC builder majority-owned by crypto-miner Bit Digital. A high-convexity "5x-or-zero" torque play on AI-compute demand, **not** a de-rated-quality holding. Fits a willing-to-lose lottery sleeve (e.g. the $200K moonshot), *not* the core book.

## One-line thesis
Micro-cap ($1.5B) neocloud — GPU cloud + colocation — IPO'd Aug 2025, growing fast off a tiny base ($21.9M quarterly revenue) but burning ~$166M/quarter of FCF against $75.8M cash, so the entire thesis is a bet on continuous external financing executing the NC-1 (Madison, NC) data-center buildout before the cash runs out.

## What they do
AI-infrastructure provider via two segments: **cloud services** (GPU/HPC for generative-AI + ML workloads) and **colocation** (space/power/cooling in its own data centers). Building the **NC-1 / Madison, North Carolina** AI/HPC campus. Spun out of / majority-owned by **Bit Digital** — the same crypto-miner-to-AI-host pattern as [[IREN]] / [[APLD]] / Core Scientific.

## Bull case
- **High-convexity torque on AI-compute demand off a tiny base.** A $1.5B mcap with a real buildout can re-rate violently if NC-1 lands anchor contracts — the kind of name that 3-5x's in an AI melt-up.
- **Real demand backdrop** — the neocloud thesis is intact (see [[ai-capex-cycle]], GPU capacity sold out); sub-scale players that execute get bought or re-rated.
- **$100M delayed-draw term loan** (expandable to $150M; Bit Digital + B. Riley) bridges near-term financing for NC-1 — buys runway.
- **Smart-money tail tell:** appears as a *tail long* in Aschenbrenner's Situational Awareness 13F (the AGI fund's neocloud basket) — see [[2026-06-20-aschenbrenner-situational-awareness]].

## Bear case / risks
- **🔴 Existential financing dependence.** ~$169M/qtr capex and ~−$166M/qtr FCF against only **$75.8M cash** = the company must *continuously* raise capital (debt or equity) just to keep building. Any financing-window slam (rates, sentiment, credit) is an existential, not a cyclical, risk.
- **🔴 Sub-scale + lossmaking.** $22M quarterly revenue and ~−45% margins; this is a tier (or two) below [[CRWV]] (~$99B backlog) and [[NBIS]] (adj-EBITDA profitable, $9.3B cash) — and below even [[IREN]]/[[APLD]]. No disclosed anchor customers.
- **🟠 Related-party + crypto-parent risk.** Majority-owned by **Bit Digital**, which is also the lender — related-party governance, and a crypto-miner parent adds crypto-price correlation/financing-cycle risk to the story.
- **🟠 Dilution/refi risk.** A debt-funded micro-cap burning cash this fast will likely return to equity markets — dilution is the base case; the term loan is a bridge, not a solution.
- **🟠 Commoditization** — GPU-rental economics compress as supply catches up; the smallest players have the least pricing power and the weakest balance sheets to survive a downturn.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **The next financing** — how NC-1 gets funded to completion, and at what dilution/coupon. The whole thesis.
2. **Anchor contracts** — any disclosed hyperscaler/IG offtake (the IREN-Microsoft-style re-rate catalyst).
3. **Cash runway** — cash vs burn each quarter; the clock is the risk.
4. **Bit Digital relationship** — terms of the related-party debt; any conflicts.
