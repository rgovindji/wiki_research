# Nokia (NOK)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** low (early-stage thesis) · **Time horizon:** long-term · **Listing:** NYSE ADR (also Helsinki)

## One-line thesis
**NVDA invested $1B** (Oct 2025 / extended) to accelerate **AI-RAN** innovation and lead the **5G→6G transition**. Long-term call option on AI-native mobile networks; T-Mobile US trials begin 2026. Core telecom equipment business is mature/cyclical and provides limited near-term torque.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **NVDA strategic partner** for AI-native mobile network infrastructure
- **NVIDIA Arc Aerial RAN Computer** — co-developed 6G-ready compute platform
- **T-Mobile US trial partnership** — field validation begins 2026
- One of the few telecom equipment names with explicit NVDA equity tie

## Bull case
- **NVDA $1B investment** validates the AI-RAN thesis
- **6G transition** is a multi-year content tailwind
- **T-Mobile field trials** in 2026 = first real-world data points
- **AI-RAN architecture** is a structural change in how mobile networks are built
- **Mature dividend / cash flow** profile from existing telecom business

## Bear case / risks
- **Slow telecom capex** — operators globally are cautious; 5G payback still maturing
- **6G is years away** at scale — revenue inflection a multi-year wait
- **Competitive pressure** from Ericsson, Samsung, Huawei
- **Macro / FX exposure** — global revenue mix
- **Execution risk** on AI-RAN transition

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **T-Mobile US trial results** through 2026
2. **AI-RAN order intake** — first commercial deployments
3. **Operator capex** trajectory globally
4. **Operating margin** as mix shifts to AI-RAN
5. **Free cash flow** trajectory
