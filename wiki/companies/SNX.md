# TD SYNNEX (SNX)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** low · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo tactical

## One-line thesis
The world's largest IT distributor — a low-margin, high-volume channel mover of hardware/software/cloud, with a thin-but-real AI tailwind from distributing AI servers and infrastructure into the enterprise channel. A **value/GARP industrial-tech** name, not an AI primary beneficiary; the AI exposure is a margin-mix kicker, not the thesis.

> **What this means:** SNX is plumbing — it moves $65B of IT product a year at razor-thin margins. AI servers flowing through the channel help volumes and mix modestly, but distribution is a spread business; SNX captures a small slice of the AI dollar, not the GPU/cloud economics that drive [[CRWV]] or [[NVDA]]. It's a cheap, steady compounder, not an AI growth name.

## Bull case
- **Scale moat in distribution** — largest IT distributor; entrenched vendor + reseller relationships are hard to displace.
- **AI-server channel tailwind** — enterprise AI infrastructure increasingly flows through distribution; supports volumes + a modest mix lift.
- **Cheap + shareholder-friendly** — ~17x forward, +20% EPS growth (FY25), buybacks/dividend; classic value/GARP.

## Bear case / risks
- **Structurally thin margins** — distribution is a spread business; AI hardware doesn't change the model's low-margin nature.
- **Cyclical IT demand** + customer/vendor concentration; rate-sensitive working-capital model ([[fed-policy]]).
- **Near 52-wk high** with analyst avg PT (~$269.82 as of 2026-06-18) *below* spot — implied downside per the Street.
- **Marginal AI relevance** — a beneficiary-by-proximity, not a driver.

## VERDICT
- **Book-fit: pass (or value-watchlist).** A solid cheap distributor with a faint AI tailwind, but it's a margin-spread business outside the wiki's AI/tech-leader focus and trades above the Street's target. Not a core add.
