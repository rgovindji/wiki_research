# Lumentum (LITE)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** high · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
Photonics leader paired with [[COHR]] in **NVDA's $4B March 2026 commitment** (split between the two). OCS (optical switching) backlog **>$400M** with multi-hundred-million-dollar CPO orders in hand.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **Photonics partner for CPO** — silicon photonics for AI scale-out fabric
- **Multi-year purchase commitment + capacity priority** from NVDA's $2B-of-$4B share
- **EML lasers** — one of the few suppliers of the upstream component constraining the rest of the optical chain
- Joint participation in **NVIDIA Spectrum-X / Quantum-X** photonics switches

## Bull case
- **NVDA $2B direct investment** = strong demand validation
- **OCS backlog >$400M** with confirmed CPO orders on top
- **EML supplier position** = pricing power upstream
- **CPO transition** is a multi-year content tailwind per AI scale-out node
- Cleaner pure-play AI exposure than [[COHR]] (less consumer-electronics business)

## Bear case / risks
- **Optics trade volatile** — May 2026 saw LITE drop ~7% on cool-off
- **Customer concentration risk** with NVDA share growing rapidly
- **CPO timing risk** — adoption curve uncertain ("scale-out first, scale-up later")
- **Competitive pressure** from COHR, Marvell, plus Asian challengers
- **Already-elevated multiple** post 2025-26 run

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **OCS / CPO order intake** quarterly
2. **EML production capacity** — does the upstream bottleneck ease or tighten?
3. **NVDA revenue mix** — disclosure trajectory
4. **Margin trajectory** — does scale flow through?
