# Qualcomm (QCOM)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
Cheap (~23x forward) edge-AI / on-device-inference proxy with a real datacenter-AI optionality narrative (Tenstorrent talks, AI200/AI250 server chips) layered on a durable handset + auto franchise — the value-priced way to own the "AI leaves the cloud" theme, with Apple-modem loss as the known offsetting overhang.

## Bull case
- **Cheapest large-cap AI-adjacent name** — ~23x forward vs. peers at 35-55x.
- **On-device / edge AI** — Snapdragon as the inference engine for AI phones, PCs, autos; 40+ AI-enabled devices in pipeline.
- **Datacenter optionality** — reported Tenstorrent acquisition talks ($8-10B) plus AI200/AI250 datacenter inference chips; a genuine new TAM if it lands.
- **Automotive + IoT diversification** reduces handset dependence; auto design-win backlog growing.

## Bear case / risks
- **Apple modem loss** — Apple's in-house modem ramp removes a major QCT revenue stream over the next 1-2 years; the central overhang.
- **Handset maturity / China exposure** — core market is cyclical and geopolitically exposed.
- **Datacenter AI is unproven** — QCOM has tried and retreated from server silicon before; execution risk is high.
- **Licensing (QTL) legal/renewal risk** — high-margin licensing is the profit engine and faces periodic disputes.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Apple-modem revenue cliff timing and magnitude.
2. Tenstorrent deal close + datacenter chip traction (AI200/AI250 design wins).
3. Auto + IoT revenue mix climbing.
4. Capital return (buyback pace) supporting the multiple.

## Verdict
Bull / medium conviction. **Book-fit: core-add candidate** — the value/contrarian edge-AI slot. The de-rate (~13% off highs) + cheap multiple + free datacenter optionality is the most asymmetric setup in Cluster A. Apple cliff is the falsifier.
