# Monolithic Power Systems (MPWR)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
Specialty PMIC (power management IC) supplier with strong AI server content — Monolithic's high-density power solutions are widely embedded in GPU power delivery for hyperscaler AI servers. Listed in NVIDIA's 800V DC architecture ecosystem alongside [[ADI]], [[STM]], [[ON]], [[NVTS]].

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **Power management for GPU and CPU rails** at the board / module level
- Critical content in dense AI server power delivery (GB200 / GB300 / Rubin)
- Listed as part of NVIDIA's broader 800V DC power architecture initiative

## Bull case
- **High AI server content** — PMIC market grows with GPU complexity and power density
- **Track record** of strong execution and consistent gross margins
- **Diversified end markets** beyond AI (auto, industrial, consumer)
- **Capital-light model** — fabless

## Bear case / risks
- **Customer concentration** — historically a small set of large customers
- **Margin pressure** if competitive intensity rises (Infineon, Renesas in PMIC)
- **AI capex cyclicality** flows through to volume directly
- **Premium multiple** — high-quality fabless pricing

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **AI / data center revenue mix** quarterly
2. **Gross margin trajectory** under competitive pressure
3. **Customer concentration disclosure**
4. **800V DC architecture content per rack**
