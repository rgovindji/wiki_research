# Eaton (ETN)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** high · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
Power management leader collaborating directly with [[NVDA]] on the **Beam Rubin DSX platform** — addressing the "nearly $7T data center buildout from grid to chip." Q4 2025 data center orders accelerated **~200%**; sales **+40%+ YoY**. Diversified beyond AI through electrical, aerospace, and vehicle segments.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **Power infrastructure from grid to chip** — UPS, switchgear, busways, racks, modular data center products
- **Beam Rubin DSX with NVIDIA** — joint reference design integrated with NVIDIA Vera Rubin DSX AI Factory
- Part of the NVIDIA **800V DC power architecture** initiative alongside ABB, Schneider, [[VRT]], Infineon, [[STM]], Navitas
- **Eaton-Flexnode partnership** — modular data center deployment cuts schedules ~35%

## Bull case
- **Direct NVDA partnership** (Beam Rubin DSX) — branded reference design status
- **Q4 2025 orders +200%; sales +40%+** — orders well ahead of revenue
- **800V DC** transition is a multi-year content tailwind
- **Diversified** — Electrical Americas, Electrical Global, Aerospace, Vehicle, eMobility provide non-AI ballast
- **Industrial / utility electrification** complementary to AI data center growth

## Bear case / risks
- **Power is NOT going to be the binding AI constraint (NEW).** Per [[2026-05-09-dwarkesh-dylan-semianalysis]]: 16+ vendors of power generation; behind-the-meter ~50% of new capacity by EOD; batteries can absorb 20% of US grid. **Implication:** ETN data center growth remains huge, but the structural-bottleneck premium investors might pay for being one of "only three turbine OEMs" doesn't apply — the alternatives ladder is deep.
- **AI capex cycle exposure** — data center segment has become a meaningful share
- **Premium multiple** — quality compounder pricing
- **Competition** — Schneider, ABB, [[VRT]]
- **Vehicle segment** macro sensitivity
- **Execution risk** on modular / rapid-deploy products

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Data center order growth** vs. broader Eaton orders
2. **Beam Rubin DSX rollout** — first deployments
3. **Aerospace + Vehicle** for non-AI direction
4. **Operating margin** in Electrical Americas segment
5. **Backlog conversion**
