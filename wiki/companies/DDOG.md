# Datadog (DDOG)

**Stance:** neutral (cut from the book on factor risk, not thesis) · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo · **Verdict:** the best of the software theses — *usage-based observability is an AI tailwind, not a target* — but it's the lone software name with the highest software-sentiment beta, so it de-rates with the complex regardless of its own fundamentals. Held off the core book for *portfolio-construction* reasons, not because the business is broken.

## One-line thesis
Observability priced on data/hosts/spans ingested (not per-seat), so "agents replace seats" doesn't hit it the way it hits horizontal SaaS — *more AI infra + more agents + more microservices = more telemetry to monitor* — but it trades at a premium multiple and moves with the software complex, which is de-rating.

## Bull case
- **AI is a tailwind, not a threat** — usage-based pricing means agent sprawl *increases* what there is to monitor; the cleanest "survives AI-eats-SaaS" profile in software (see [[ai-software-disruption]]).
- **~70%+ gross margins hold** because cost scales slower than usage; AI-native revenue growing >100% off a >10% base.
- De-rated ~20% off its high with the software complex — entry is more reasonable than a year ago.

## Bear case / risks
- **🔴 Highest software-sentiment beta in a de-rating complex.** It moves *with* CRM/ADBE/NOW regardless of its own numbers; in the June software de-rate it sold off on the SaaSpocalypse fear (see [[ai-bubble-debate]]).
- **🟠 Premium multiple (~22x sales)** — compresses on sentiment alone in a "software de-rate" regime.
- **🟠 Observability could itself be disrupted** — AI agents monitoring AI (self-healing), or hyperscalers/LLM platforms bundling observability into the stack.
- **🟠 Lone software name** in an otherwise compute/power picks-and-shovels book — odd-one-out fit (the reason it was cut from v3/v4).

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Does AI-native revenue keep compounding >100% and become a clear growth driver (not just a line item)?
2. Does the software-complex de-rate stabilize, or is this a generational multiple reset?
3. Any sign hyperscalers are bundling observability and compressing the TAM?
