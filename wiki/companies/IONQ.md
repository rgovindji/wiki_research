## Role in AI / quantum
- **Trapped-ion qubit modality**: longer coherence times than superconducting (Rigetti/IBM/Google) but slower gate operations
- **Algorithmic Qubit (AQ) metric**: IONQ-developed benchmark; recent systems claim AQ#36 or higher
- **AWS Braket + Microsoft Azure**: customers access via cloud (vs on-premise deployment) = low-friction adoption
- **Quantum advantage / fault-tolerance**: still 2-3 years out for usable workloads

## Bull case

- **2026 guide $260-270M** = 100% organic growth maintaining
- **AWS Braket + Microsoft Azure** = cloud-first distribution; no on-premise lock-in needed
- **Trapped-ion advantage in coherence** = better for certain algorithm classes vs superconducting

- **Trapped-ion gate speed disadvantage** vs superconducting — could lose on practical workloads
- **Competition intensifying**: IBM Nighthawk, Google Willow, Microsoft Majorana-1, Rigetti Lyra
- **Quantum error correction milestones unproven at commercial scale** — Microsoft + Atom Computing fault-tolerant system mid-2026 is the test
