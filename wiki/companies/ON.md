# onsemi (ON)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
SiC (silicon carbide) and power-management leader, listed in NVIDIA's **800V DC power architecture** ecosystem — but core auto SiC business is in cyclical downturn, limiting near-term financial leverage from AI exposure.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **800V DC power architecture partner** alongside Infineon, Innoscience, MPS, [[NVTS]], [[ADI]], ROHM, [[STM]], TI
- **SiC** for high-efficiency power conversion in AI factories
- **Power management ICs** for server / rack-level power

## Bull case
- **SiC industry leader** with East Fishkill capacity expansion
- **AI server content growth** as 800V transition rolls out
- **Auto recovery + AI exposure** = potential dual tailwind
- **Long-term EV trend** for SiC remains intact
- **Diversified industrial + auto + cloud** end markets

## Bear case / risks
- **Auto SiC market** in cyclical downturn — primary near-term headwind
- **Inventory correction** still working through
- **AI revenue is a smaller mix** than auto / industrial
- **Competitive intensity** in SiC (STM, Wolfspeed, Infineon)
- **Wolfspeed restructuring** noise affecting industry sentiment

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Auto SiC trough** — when does it inflect?
2. **AI / data center revenue mix** disclosure
3. **East Fishkill ramp** and utilization
4. **Operating margin trajectory** through the recovery
