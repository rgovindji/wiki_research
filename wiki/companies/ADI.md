# Analog Devices (ADI)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
High-quality analog and signal processing franchise listed in NVIDIA's **800V DC power architecture** ecosystem — content per AI server is rising as power, signal integrity, and clock distribution become more demanding. Diversified end markets (industrial, auto, healthcare) provide ballast.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **800V DC power architecture partner** alongside Infineon, Innoscience, MPS, Navitas, OnSemi, ROHM, STMicroelectronics, TI
- **Power management ICs** for AI server power chains
- **Signal-chain content** — clocks, drivers, PMICs in AI infrastructure

## Bull case
- **AI infrastructure content growth** per server / rack
- **Diversified end markets** (industrial, auto, healthcare) provide cyclical ballast
- **Quality compounder** profile — high gross margins, strong FCF
- **Industrial recovery** tailwind on top of AI

## Bear case / risks
- **AI is a smaller revenue mix** vs. industrial / auto
- **Industrial cycle** still digesting prior excess
- **Auto cycle exposure**
- **Premium multiple** — quality compounder pricing limits margin of safety
- **Acquisition integration** (Maxim) — execution maturity

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Industrial recovery** — direction quarter-over-quarter
2. **AI / data center content disclosure**
3. **Operating margin trajectory** through the recovery
4. **800V DC product traction**
