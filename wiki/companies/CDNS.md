# Cadence Design Systems (CDNS)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term + tactical

> **Why a page now (2026-05-17):** wiki has covered [[SNPS]] as part of the EDA duopoly bull case, but [[CDNS]] has been a coverage gap. The TSMC-Cadence partnership announcement shows Cadence is matching every Synopsys move at TSMC's advanced nodes — meaning the EDA duopoly bull case applies to both names equally. Stub-quality coverage; deepen on user signal.

## One-line thesis
Cadence is the co-equal half of the EDA duopoly with [[SNPS]], capturing the same structural design-bottleneck rent ([[2026-05-11-semianalysis-eda-primer]]) — chip complexity grows ~50%/yr vs. design productivity only ~20%/yr, the gap is widening every generation, and only AI-driven EDA tools close it. **Which means** CDNS has the same compounder thesis as SNPS at similar (~3-5pp lower) valuations.

## Role in the AI silicon stack
Cadence + [[SNPS]] = the EDA duopoly. Every advanced-node chip (N3, N2, A16, A14, A13) is designed with tools from one or both of them. Customer base is the entire AI accelerator industry: [[NVDA]], [[AMD]], [[AVGO]], MRVL, plus every hyperscaler custom silicon program.

## Bull case
- **EDA duopoly with [[SNPS]]** — pricing discipline; subscription / recurring revenue model; cyclicality much lower than hardware peers
- **Same structural compounder thesis as [[SNPS]]** — per [[2026-05-11-semianalysis-eda-primer]]: chip complexity grows ~50%/yr; design productivity grows ~20%/yr; **verification = up to 70% of total project effort**; the only way to close the gap is more EDA + AI automation
- **Deep TSMC alliance (NEW 2026-05-17)** — per Daniel Nenni article "TSMC and Cadence Strengthen Partnership": Cadence supports TSMC N3/N2/A16; **joint development on A14**; PDK readiness early; AI-driven design flows. **COUPE photonics support via Virtuoso Studio + Celsius Thermal Solver** = direct read-through to [[SOITEC]] CPO substrate thesis
- **3DFabric / 3D-IC**: Cadence 3D-IC solutions support TSMC's full advanced packaging stack (bump connections, multi-chiplet physical implementation, system-level analysis); **Clarity 3D Solver + Sigrity X** for signal/power/thermal integrity
- **Silicon-proven IP on N3P**: **HBM4, LPDDR6/5X, DDR5 MRDIMM Gen2, PCIe 7.0, UCIe** — full memory + interconnect IP portfolio
- **Open Innovation Platform** ecosystem with TSMC — long-term moat
- **Lower NVDA-direct-investment exposure than [[SNPS]]** (which has NVDA $2B AI-EDA partnership) — could be a relative bear, but **CDNS isn't dependent on a single hyperscaler relationship for AI exposure**

## Bear case / risks
- **Premium multiple** — EDA always trades expensive
- **[[SNPS]] has the NVDA $2B partnership** — Synopsys may capture larger share of "agentic AI" EDA TAM if that becomes the differentiator
- **Customer concentration** in advanced semis
- **Ansys integration is at SNPS, not CDNS** — SNPS expanded multiphysics; CDNS has Cadence-internal equivalents (Clarity, Sigrity, Celsius) but no transformational M&A in the same window
- **Slowing chip startup ecosystem** — EDA seat growth at risk if VC funding cools
- **Antitrust scrutiny** on EDA consolidation (applies to both duopoly members)
- **RVA / RISC-V open-tool emergence** — long-term threat that affects both CDNS and SNPS
