## Role in AI / SMR
- **First-of-a-kind commercial SMR** in the US targeting late 2027/early 2028
- **Liquid-metal fast reactor**: passive safety, high temperature, no water moderation
- **Build-Own-Operate model** (vs traditional utility selling power) — capture more economics
- **Designed for data center co-location**: smaller footprint, modular, scalable

## Bull case
- **Earliest commercial deployment** of any US SMR developer (vs NuScale 2030, X-energy 2032-2035, Kairos 2030)
- **Sam Altman backing**: chairman + significant equity holder; OpenAI ecosystem AI-power demand alignment
- **NRC PDC review on HALF traditional timeline** (early 2026) — regulatory acceleration is real
- **Meta 6.6 GW target across Oklo / Vistra / TerraPower** — anchor customer demand
- **Atomic Alchemy subsidiary** got first NRC materials license = real regulatory milestone (de-risking)
- **Build-Own-Operate captures more economics** than traditional utility sales model
- **2026-2030 sentiment window**: X-energy IPO success (April 2026 $1B raise + 31% pop) validates SMR retail enthusiasm
- **Pre-construction phase** with multiple customer agreements (NGOs, defense facilities, Equinix data centers)

## Bear case / risks
- **First-of-a-kind construction risk** — historical FOAK reactors run 30-200% over budget + 2-5 yrs over schedule
- **NRC approval still 18-24 months away** for COLA (Combined License Application)
- **Pre-revenue** — no commercial cash flow until 2028+ if Aurora hits target
- **Scaling beyond pilot unproven** — even if Aurora succeeds, ramp to commercial volume is 3-5 yrs after first criticality
- **Liquid-metal fast reactor technology**: more complex than light-water; corrosion + materials challenges historically constrain
- **Sam Altman political risks**: any OpenAI controversy bleeds into OKLO sentiment
- **Stock has been speculative high-beta** — major drawdowns on no specific news typical
- **Arnold "10-15 years" framing**: at scale, SMRs don't materially displace grid power until 2033-2035 — OKLO is the first-pilot bet, not the at-scale bet
- **Competition for data center anchor customers**: Constellation TMI, Vistra Meta, X-energy Amazon, Kairos Google all chasing same hyperscaler PPAs
