# Flex Ltd (FLEX)

**Stance:** bull · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** long-term

## One-line thesis
Diversified contract manufacturer (formerly Flextronics) with growing AI / data center power infrastructure exposure — adjacent to [[JBL]] but with a broader end-market mix providing some non-AI ballast.

## Role in the NVDA stack
- **Contract manufacturing** for AI server OEMs and hyperscaler-direct programs
- **Power infrastructure** for data centers — power cabinets, rack-scale power solutions
- More diversified end-market mix than [[JBL]] (industrial, automotive, healthcare in addition to cloud)

## Bull case
- **AI infra demand pull-through** — content per data center growing
- **Diversified end markets** — less concentrated than JBL on hyperscaler AI
- **Operating leverage** if AI mix grows margin
- **Industrial / automotive / healthcare** as non-AI ballast

## Bear case / risks
- **Lower AI exposure / growth rate** than JBL
- **Contract manufacturing margins** are structurally thin
- **Customer concentration** in select segments
- **Less coverage / disclosure** of AI-specific revenue mix
- **Macro sensitivity** in industrial/automotive end markets

## Key questions / what to watch
1. **Cloud / data center segment** mix and growth
2. **AI-attributable revenue** disclosure (currently limited)
3. **Operating margin trajectory**
4. **Industrial / automotive cycle** read
