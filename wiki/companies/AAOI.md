# Applied Optoelectronics (AAOI)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** low · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo (speculative photonics-cycle play, watch/size-small)

## One-line thesis
A US-based vertically-integrated optical-transceiver maker riding the 800G→1.6T hyperscaler buildout into a record-revenue inflection — but a small-cap with a documented boom-bust history, customer concentration, and a stock already up ~12x off its lows, so the cycle is largely priced.

At **$15.37B market cap on ~$1.1B FY26 revenue guide, that's ~14x forward sales** — a CPO-disruptable module maker priced like a structural winner. The valuation *is* the bear case.

## What they do
Designs and manufactures optical components and transceivers — vertically integrated (lasers → components → modules) — for three markets: **data center** (the AI growth engine: 100G/200G/400G/800G, ramping 1.6T), **CATV/broadband**, and telecom. US-based manufacturing (Texas) is a differentiator in a China-heavy optical supply chain. Sits at the **pluggable optical module** layer of the [[photonics]] stack alongside [[COHR]], [[LITE]], [[FN]].

## Bull case
- **800G→1.6T hyperscaler inflection.** Data center revenue +154% y/y; first 800G volume shipment to a major hyperscaler in Q1; **first 1.6T volume order (>$200M) announced March 2026**. Capacity scaling toward ~700k units/month of 800G+1.6T.
- **Microsoft multi-year supply agreement** structurally lifted gross margin (data-center mix now >70% of revenue, higher-margin). Amazon 800G demand + expected Oracle qualification per Rosenblatt (PT raised $140→$220, Jun 4 2026).
- **US-based manufacturing** is a geopolitical/customer-diversification tailwind (same theme as the [[INTC]] foundry and US-onshoring trades).
- Pure-play leverage to the [[photonics]] / optical-interconnect TAM the wiki tracks as a power-constrained AI bottleneck.

## Bear case / risks
- **"Dark history."** AAOI has been here before — a 2017 Amazon/Microsoft-driven boom that collapsed on customer loss and inventory; a multi-year history of dilution (frequent equity/convert raises) and volatile margins. Pattern-match to the small-cap-rerate caution in [[ai-bubble-debate]].
- **Stock up ~12x off the low; cycle largely priced.** Q1 beat *guidance* but **missed consensus** ($151M vs ~$157M est) even at a record — expectations have run ahead of delivery.
- **Customer concentration.** Hyperscaler design wins drive the upside and the downside; a single qualification slip or order push compresses the model fast.
- **Technology-transition risk.** The pluggable franchise that's ramping today (800G/1.6T modules) is the layer **co-packaged optics (CPO)** eventually disrupts — see [[photonics]]. AAOI is levered to the module layer specifically.
- **CATV is a legacy drag** — roughly half of revenue is lower-growth broadband, diluting the AI-optics narrative.

## Key questions / what to watch
- Does 800G/1.6T volume convert the $324M+ backlog into the guided $1.1B FY2026 — and do gross margins hold as the mix shifts?
- Oracle and additional hyperscaler qualifications — do they land?
- Any equity/convert raise into the strength (the historical dilution signature; watch EDGAR like the [[VELO]] / [[POET]] rug-check).
- 1.6T ramp timing vs CPO encroachment at the leading edge.
