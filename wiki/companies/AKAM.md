# Akamai Technologies (AKAM)

**Stance:** neutral · **Conviction:** medium · **Time horizon:** 6-18mo

## One-line thesis
A cheap (~18x forward), de-rated (~24% off highs) legacy-CDN business being restructured into a security + edge-compute + AI-inference-at-the-edge story; the value/turnaround setup is real, but the AI angle is optionality on top of a slow-growth (+6% rev) core, not a primary AI growth engine.

## Bull case
- **Cheap + de-rated** — ~18x forward, ~24% off the 52-wk high; a value entry vs. the AI peer group.
- **Mix shift to security + compute** — Security and Cloud Computing segments grow double digits and now carry the story as legacy delivery (CDN) shrinks.
- **Edge-inference optionality** — Akamai's globally distributed edge network is a natural home for low-latency AI inference; partnership/product motion here is the upside call.
- **Strong FCF + buybacks** support the floor while the mix shifts.

## Bear case / risks
- **Legacy CDN decline** — delivery revenue is in secular decline; the whole thesis rests on security/compute outgrowing the drag, which keeps total growth at only ~6%.
- **Edge-inference is unproven revenue** — a narrative, not yet a material line item; hyperscalers compete directly.
- **Competitive security market** — Cloudflare, Zscaler, and platform vendors crowd the space.
- **Value-trap risk** — cheap multiple can stay cheap if growth never re-accelerates.

## Key questions / what to watch
1. Security + Cloud Computing segment growth vs. delivery decline — the crossover.
2. Any concrete edge-AI-inference revenue or marquee customer.
3. Total revenue re-acceleration above high-single-digits.
4. Margin/FCF stability through the transition.

## Verdict
Neutral / medium conviction. **Book-fit: watchlist** (value/edge-AI optionality slot). Already referenced in the book + index, so the page closes a gap. Cheap and de-risked, but the AI thesis is optionality on a slow core — not a core AI holding.
