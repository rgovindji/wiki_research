## The next sector to watch: **AI-Security / cybersecurity**
Forward hypothesis (not yet a high-conviction wiki thesis) — the strongest catalyst stack of any adjacent sector:
- **Budget shift:** Gartner — AI-tied cyber spend **8% (2023) → 40% (2027)**; ~2/3 of orgs raising cyber spend in 2026 (1-in-4 by >25%).
- **New attack surface:** agentic AI = "most consequential unsecured asset"; models can autonomously find/exploit vulns (the Claude-Mythos export-control concern; see [[2026-06-30-uncoveralpha-token-optimization-hyperscalers]]).
- **Regulatory tailwind:** the June-2 2026 EO + Treasury cyber clearinghouse (a spending forcing-function).
- **Consolidation** favors scaled platforms (PANW +44% in a month = the tape confirming).
- **Entry caveat:** the leaders ([[CRWD]] +11%, [[PANW]] +18%) already ran — watch dips or the second tier (ZS, S/SentinelOne, FTNT) + the AI-edge angle ([[NET]]).
