# Bank of Japan + Yen Carry Trade

## What this is
The Bank of Japan (BoJ) rate path is the **single highest-leverage macro decision point for global risk assets in 2026** because of the yen carry trade — leveraged short-yen positions funding long-duration assets (US tech, EM equities, commodities). A BoJ hike triggers yen strength which forces carry trade unwinds, which trigger forced selling in high-duration assets.

## Why this matters for the wiki
- The wiki is concentrated in **high-multiple US AI/tech** ([[NVDA]] 280x forward, [[MU]] +154% YTD, [[CRWV]] / [[NBIS]] premium growth, [[SOITEC]] +447% YTD) — exactly the asset class that gets sold first in a duration shock
- **Japanese institutional holders** (GPIF $1.8T, life insurers, Norinchukin) own meaningful percentages of [[NVDA]], [[MSFT]], [[AAPL]], [[GOOGL]]; repatriation flow on yen strength = forced US tech selling
- [[hedging-risk]] page tracks tail-risk instruments; BoJ is the most-actionable scheduled catalyst risk in 2026
- This is the macro lever **most disconnected from the AI capex / earnings narrative** the rest of the wiki tracks — and therefore the cleanest hedge insight

## Why 2026 unwind risk > 2024

1. **Less BoJ retreat room.** Aug 2024 BoJ raised 0.25%, Uchida walked back hawkishness within **8 days**. After 2 more years of yen weakness + accumulating import inflation + 6-3 hawkish-leaning vote, that retreat is politically harder. Credibility cost has risen.
2. **Position rebuilt.** Per MS, ~90% of pre-Aug-2024 carry trade size remains. 2024 was a partial relief.
3. **No automatic Fed differential support.** In Aug 2024, Fed was about to cut (Sept 25bp delivered). In 2026, Fed pause / re-cut uncertainty is live. If BoJ hikes while Fed holds, sustained differential pressure.
4. **Multiples higher.** NVDA 280x forward PE (May 2026) vs ~50x early 2024. Same vol shock = bigger drawdown.
5. **GPIF rebalancing.** $1.8T fund has internal discussion to **reduce foreign bonds + equities**. Yen strength accelerates this. Slow-burn flow risk that didn't exist in 2024.

## August 2024 reference event

For calibration:
- **Trigger**: BoJ +15bp July 31 + weak US jobs Aug 2
- **Peak chaos**: Aug 5, 6 AM ET — VIX **+180% intraday to 65** (largest single-day spike on record); NIKKEI **-12.4%** in one day (worst since 1987)
- **Resolution**: 4 trading days. S&P 500 fully recovered by Aug 9. VIX receded.
- **Why fast**: Uchida hawkish-walk-back Aug 7; Fed cut Sept; positioning reset

The Aug 2024 episode is the **lower bound** for what a 2026 unwind looks like. Most analysts (BCA, SocGen, BIS) argue 2026 conditions are worse.

## Open questions

- **Path beyond June**: if BoJ hikes 25bp June, does the swaps curve price 50bp by Sept? That's the path-dependent risk
- **Iran war energy shock duration**: BoJ April inflation upgrade was Iran-driven. If oil normalizes, inflation cover for further BoJ hikes weakens
- **PM Ishiba political stability**: a snap election would freeze BoJ
- **GPIF actual rebalancing**: discussion vs disclosed allocation shifts — has anything actually moved?
- **Fed September meeting interaction**: if Fed cuts while BoJ hikes, differential pressure could exceed Aug 2024
- **Ueda communication tone**: any speech between now and June 16 is the leading indicator

## Falsifiable watch triggers

- **USD/JPY breaks 145 ahead of June 16-17**: BoJ doesn't need to hike (yen corrected on its own); meeting is non-event
- **USD/JPY stays above 158 into June 16-17**: hike pressure intensifies; tail risk lives
- **NIKKEI / VIX behavior 24-48hrs ahead of meeting**: front-running vol = position-clearing happening
- **BoJ Ueda speech tone post-G7 meetings**: any hawkish drift is the leading edge
- **Shunto 2027 settlement preliminaries (autumn 2026)**: wage growth trajectory determines BoJ inflation cover into 2027
