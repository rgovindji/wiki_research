Open Questions / Unresolved

  • Does ASML finally take pricing power in 2028-2030? Historically the "generous monopolist" — supply-constrained, not commercially constrained. Single highest-leverage variable for the multi-year semi thesis.
  • Does SNDK HBF (High-Bandwidth Flash) ship and scale? If NAND-based vertical memory genuinely substitutes for HBM at 8-16x capacity, it's an architectural threat to the HBM supercycle thesis on a 2-3 year horizon.
  • When does the first hyperscaler cut AI capex guidance? The single most important leading indicator of a cycle peak.
  • How to size NVDA + GOOGL + MSFT + META given correlated AI exposure? Owning all four is one bet, not four.
  • If long-AI timelines (2035+), does China dominate semis? Their indigenized DUV is coming; EUV later but eventually. Fast-timelines US wins; long-timelines China wins.
  • Is the trade rotating from premium consumer tech to pure AI infrastructure? AAPL bear flip + smartphone volume collapse + AI capex shift all point in that direction.
