# PIC production scaling: NewPhotonics adopts yieldHUB analytics

## TL;DR
- **Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) production is scaling out of the lab** across datacom, telecom, AI infrastructure, sensing, and quantum computing — enough that manufacturers now need semiconductor-grade yield analytics.
- **NewPhotonics** (private PIC maker) adopted **yieldHUB's** analytics platform as a core part of its manufacturing/quality strategy — a small but real signal that PIC volume manufacturing is maturing.

## Key facts
- PIC manufacturing reuses much of standard wafer fab but adds **optical-specific variables**: waveguide uniformity, coupling efficiency, material interactions, packaging tolerances. These are new yield detractors electrical chips don't have.
- As volumes rise, manufacturers need centralized data + root-cause analytics across the flow — i.e., PIC is hitting the industrialization phase where yield engineering becomes the gating discipline.

## Key claims (and confidence)
- Vendor-PR in form (yieldHUB customer story), but the **underlying signal is real and net-new for the wiki**: a named PIC manufacturer at volume + yield being the bottleneck = the photonics-scaling thesis showing up in the boring operational layer where real ramps actually show first.
- **NewPhotonics** is a diamond-in-the-rough name to track (private; no public access yet).

## Diamond-in-the-rough details
- yieldHUB itself is a private yield-analytics vendor (not investable) but its PIC customer count is a proxy for how many PIC makers are reaching volume.
- "Yield is the gating variable" is the operational tell for when a frontier technology becomes a real product — worth watching as the photonics ramp indicator.
