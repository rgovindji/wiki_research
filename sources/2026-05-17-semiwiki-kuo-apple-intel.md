# Ming-Chi Kuo on Apple-Intel partnership, with TSMC lurking in the back

Thread started May 14, 2026 by NY_Sam2 — reposting Ming-Chi Kuo's 10 industry checks on Apple's emerging Intel relationship. 22 replies across 2 pages; key contributors: NY_Sam2, blueone, brady, daniel-nenni, depthfirst, hist78, jorgequinonez, mkwventures, siliconbruh999, essiah.

## TL;DR
- **Apple is actively cultivating Intel as a long-term key supplier** — iPhone, iPad, and Mac processors kicked off on Intel 18A-P
- Lifecycle plan: **small-scale testing 2026 → ramp 2027 → growth 2028 → decline 2029** at Intel
- **TSMC retains over 90% of supply share** even if Intel ships smoothly
- **Apple began Intel discussions before TSMC capacity became tight** — strategic diversification, not reactive
- **Apple explicitly recognizes TSMC will continue tilting toward AI** — directly validates [[AAPL]] bear thesis on TSMC deprioritization
