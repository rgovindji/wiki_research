# SK Hynix approaching $1T market cap on HBM cycle

## TL;DR
- SK Hynix market cap stood at **~$942B Thursday May 14 close**; +200% YTD 2026 (on top of +274% in 2025); about $50B from $1T milestone.
- Holds **~60% of global HBM market**, has secured supply contracts for NVDA Blackwell + next-gen platforms; Q1 2026 operating margin **72%** — single-quarter record for the global chip industry.
- If SK Hynix crosses $1T, South Korea becomes the first country outside the US with more than one trillion-dollar company (Samsung already crossed).
- Samsung Electronics +3% to record on AI demand; Samsung Korean union planning **18-day strike from May 21** over bonus-pay gap with SK Hynix — could disrupt HBM4 ramp at Samsung.
