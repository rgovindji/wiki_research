# CoreWeave (CRWV) Q1 2026 Earnings — Source Summary

## TL;DR

- **Revenue $2.078B (+112% YoY)** — beat consensus; Q4 2025 was $1.58B implied (+32% QoQ)
- **Revenue backlog $99.4B** (~50% QoQ growth) — **2x NBIS's $44.4B** combined contracted
- **Adjusted EBITDA $1.157B** (+91% YoY, 56% margin) — adjusted operating income only $21M (margin compression on capex)
- **Operating cash flow $2.984B in one quarter**; **capex $7.7B** — massive build-out velocity
- **Anthropic multi-year deal for Claude** is a NEW disclosure (most material customer win this quarter)
- **Meta added new $21B commitment** in March 2026 (on top of prior $35B-class contracts)
- **Active power >1 GW; contracted >3.5 GW; 2030 target 8+ GW**
- **NVDA $2B Class A placement + 5+ GW partnership by 2030**
- **2026 guide $12-13B; exit 2026 at $18-19B ARR; >$30B ARR by EOY 2027**
- Q1 stock reaction: positive, validating the backlog inflection

## Why this is structurally important

CRWV's Q1 2026 print is **directly parallel to NBIS Q1 2026** (which I just processed). Both Neoclouds reported:
- Triple-digit revenue growth
- Operating cash flow inflection
- Massive backlog expansion
- New hyperscaler-scale contracts disclosed

But the magnitudes differ: CRWV is materially larger (revenue $2.078B vs NBIS $399M = ~5x; backlog $99.4B vs $44.4B = ~2.2x). Both are profitable on Adj EBITDA. **The Neocloud category as a whole is breaking out from "speculative AI infra" framing to "hyperscaler-quality scaled infrastructure" framing.**
