# TSMC raises 2030 global chip market outlook from $1T to $1.5T

## TL;DR
- TSMC raised its 2030 global semiconductor market forecast from a prior $1T baseline to **$1.5T** — a 50% upward revision in the company's anchor multi-year demand framework.
- **AI + HPC = 55% of the $1.5T** (~$825B); smartphones 20% (~$300B); automotive 10% (~$150B); remaining 15% other.
- TSMC building **9 wafer fab phases + advanced packaging facilities in 2026**; **1.8x year-on-year increase in Arizona output by year-end** with yields comparable to Taiwan.

## Key facts
- 2030 global semi market outlook: $1.5T (up from prior $1T anchor)
- AI/HPC mix at 2030: 55%
- Smartphones at 2030: 20%
- Automotive at 2030: 10%
- Arizona output expansion: 1.8x YoY in 2026
- New facility build-out: 9 wafer fab phases + advanced packaging in 2026

## Key claims and confidence
- **High confidence**: TSMC's official forward-looking framework explicitly raised. This is anchor data from the company itself.
- **High confidence**: AI/HPC at 55% reframes the multi-year AI-capex narrative — historical bear arguments anchored to $1T 2030 are now $0.5T off the mark on the upside.
- **Medium confidence**: Arizona yield parity with Taiwan is mgmt claim; production data not independently verified.
