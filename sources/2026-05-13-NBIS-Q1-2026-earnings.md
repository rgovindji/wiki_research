# Nebius (NBIS) Q1 2026 Earnings — Source Summary

## TL;DR

- **Revenue +684% YoY to $399M** (vs $388.57M consensus); **Adj EPS $2.11 vs -$0.78 expected** (beat by $2.89)
- **Profitability inflection: Adj EBITDA $129.5M vs -$53.7M Y/Y.** Operating cash flow turned massively positive at **$2.26B** (vs -$184M Y/Y).
- **Cash position $9.3B** — well-capitalized for the ~$2.5B/quarter capex run rate
- New 1.2 GW Pennsylvania AI factory + Independence MO facility; **Clarifai team acquihire + Eigen AI acquisition** to strengthen Token Factory inference platform
- Stock **+14.7% premarket**

**Which means:** the Q1 print is not just revenue scaling — it's *operating-leverage-flipping*. Every cost line as a % of revenue collapsed dramatically. This is the textbook setup for a Neocloud emerging from build-phase into harvest-phase.

## Key claims (and confidence)

- **Claim:** Profitability inflection is sustainable. → **Medium-high confidence.** Operating cash flow turning to +$2.26B is hard to reverse short of customer churn or pricing collapse. But Adj Net Loss of -$100.3M (worsened YoY) shows GAAP loss path still has work to do.
- **Claim:** Customer concentration is the biggest risk. → **High confidence.** MSFT + Meta represent ~the entire disclosed backlog. Loss of either re-rates the valuation.
- **Claim:** Capex pace is sustainable given $9.3B cash. → **Medium confidence.** $2.5B/quarter capex run-rate = ~$10B annual. Cash + operating CF + debt capacity covers this for 18-24 months, but the cycle eventually requires re-financing or equity raise.
- **Claim:** Eigen AI + Clarifai inference platform expansion is strategic, not just defensive. → **Low-medium confidence.** Token Factory positioning is real but the specific revenue trajectory is opaque from the press release alone — need the earnings call transcript.
