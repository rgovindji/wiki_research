- **Revenue $81.6B (+85% YoY, +20% QoQ)** vs $78-79B consensus = BEAT $2.6-3.6B

- **Q2 FY27 guide $91.0B ±2%** vs $85-87B consensus / $90B whisper = **BEAT THE WHISPER**

## Key claims (and confidence)

### High-confidence claims
- **AI demand accelerating, not digesting** — "parabolic" is the management characterization; Q2 guide raise of $4-6B vs whisper is empirical confirmation
- **Networking is now the second AI leg** — +199% YoY at $14.8B = third-largest segment globally and growing faster than compute
- **Vera Rubin is real and shipping** — samples are with customers; production H2 FY27 is contractual visibility, not narrative
- **Inference token economics are profitable** — Jensen explicitly: "tokens are now profitable, driving a race among model makers to produce more inference capacity"
- **Pricing power confirmed empirically** — H100 rentals +20% YTD, A100 +15%; not stagnant or declining
- **China revenue is officially off the FY27 books** — Kress: "we are uncertain whether any imports will be allowed"; this means any thaw is pure upside

### Medium-confidence claims
- **Vera CPU $200B TAM** — Jensen's claim; depends on actual ARM-server adoption velocity in 2027-2028
- **Sovereign AI 40 countries / $50T GDP** — real but lumpy; some of these are LOIs not contracted revenue
- **GB300/NVL72 hundreds of thousands of units per hyperscaler** — qualitative; not separately disclosed
