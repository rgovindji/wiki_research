## Thesis, distilled (the load-bearing claims)
1. **AGI ~2027** via continued scaling (~0.5 OOM/yr compute) + algorithmic gains (~0.5 OOM/yr) + "unhobbling." GPT-2‚ÜíGPT-4 = preschooler‚Üísmart-HS in 4 yrs; repeat by 2027.
2. **Intelligence explosion:** AGI automates AI research ‚Üí compresses ~a decade (5+ OOMs) of algo progress into <1 yr ‚Üí superintelligence shortly after.
3. **$1T+ annual AI capex / trillion-dollar individual clusters** by late decade ‚Äî "most extraordinary techno-capital acceleration." Boardroom plans escalating $10B‚Üí$100B‚Üí$1T every ~6 months.
4. **Power is THE constraint** ‚Äî "fierce scramble to secure every power contract for the rest of the decade"; US electricity must grow tens of percent. This is the part the fund trades hardest.
5. **Nationalization / "The Project":** by 2027/28 the USG runs an AGI Manhattan Project; superintelligence = decisive military/economic edge; US-CCP race (best case) or war (worst case); secrecy/espionage at labs is a live risk.

## Bias notes (read before using)
- **Aschenbrenner talks his book at maximum volume.** The essay is a long-AGI/long-compute/long-power manifesto from someone who then built a fund to monetize exactly that. Treat the thesis as a *position*, not neutral analysis.
- **Returns are self/secondary-reported and absurd (+2,065%).** No audit seen. Possible survivorship/notional-vs-NAV confusion. Do not anchor on these.
- **The raw transcript is fan-ish secondary commentary** (Bankless, crypto-long hosts), explicitly "we're all still long." Not Leopold's words.
- **Internal tension to flag:** essay says "compute buildout = trillions" but the fund is *short the GPU makers*. Reconcilable (buildout real, GPU margins compress, value accrues to power/memory/neoclouds) but worth holding as an open question, not a settled view.

## Falsifiers / bear
- AGI-2027 timeline slips (scaling plateaus, data wall, unhobbling stalls) ‚Üí the entire urgency/capex thesis deflates.
- **The short book is the most fragile part:** if NVDA/semis keep compounding (Blackwell/Rubin cycle, pricing power holds), $8.46B of puts bleed/blow up. Being short the best-performing AI asset is a high-conviction, high-fragility bet.
- Power constraint solved faster/cheaper than expected (grid, SMRs, gas) ‚Üí erodes the scarcity premium on his power longs.
- Neoclouds (CRWV/NBIS/IREN) carry lease/debt/customer-concentration risk ‚Äî exactly the leverage that hurts in a capex-cycle wobble.
