# NVIDIA × IREN — 5 GW DSX AI Factory partnership + $2.1B equity option

> **Ingest note (2026-05-31):** This announcement (May 7, 2026) was not captured in real-time by the daily-update workflow — caught on the weekend discovery sweep. Backfilled here for completeness because it's structurally relevant to NVDA's [[nvda-supply-chain]] equity-investment thesis and the neocloud-bypass-of-hyperscalers narrative.

## TL;DR

- **NVIDIA and IREN Limited (formerly Iris Energy) announced a strategic partnership May 7, 2026** to deploy up to **5 gigawatts of NVIDIA DSX-aligned AI infrastructure** across IREN's global pipeline.
- **Equity sweetener: IREN issued NVDA a 5-year right to purchase up to 30M IREN ordinary shares at $70/share** — a right to invest up to **~$2.1 billion**, subject to regulatory conditions.
- **Flagship deployment: IREN's 2 GW Sweetwater (Texas) campus** as the lead DSX site.

## Key facts

- **Scale:** Up to 5 GW deployment "over time" — comparable scale to OpenAI's Project Titan ([[AVGO]]) at 10 GW by 2029, but on a single dedicated infrastructure-partner platform.
- **Equity structure:** Warrant (not direct purchase) — gives NVDA optionality without immediate capital deployment. Strike at $70 vs. IREN trading roughly mid-teens at announcement = deeply out-of-the-money but locked in for 5 years.
- **Framework:** NVIDIA's "DSX AI factory" architecture (reference design from GTC 2026) combined with IREN's power, land, data-center, GPU-deployment expertise.
- **Customer focus:** "AI-native, startup and enterprise customers" — explicitly NOT just hyperscalers. Positioned as the neocloud bypass layer.
- **Flagship campus:** Sweetwater, Texas — 2 GW capacity at one site = larger than most operating AI campuses today.

## Key claims (and how confident)

- **High confidence:** The partnership exists; the warrant terms are public; Sweetwater is the flagship site (per NVIDIA press release direct quote).
- **High confidence:** This is part of NVIDIA's broader strategy of taking equity positions in neocloud / specialized-cloud customers — already seen with [[CRWV]] (~7% stake), [[NBIS]] (~8.3% stake), and now IREN.
- **Medium confidence:** "Up to 5 GW" is aspirational, not contracted — execution depends on IREN raising capital, securing power, building campuses on schedule.
- **Lower confidence:** "AI-native, startup and enterprise" customer mix implies NVIDIA is building a tier of compute supply for the GenAI app layer outside the Mag 7 hyperscaler oligopoly — directionally plausible given Anthropic, OpenAI, xAI compute needs but no specific customer commitments disclosed.
