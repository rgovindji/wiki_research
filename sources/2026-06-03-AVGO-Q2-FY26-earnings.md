# Broadcom (AVGO) Q2 FY2026 — strong print, stock down 12.6% on AI-guide vs. whisper

## TL;DR
- **Beat on the quarter, sold off hard.** Rev $22.2B (+48% YoY), non-GAAP EPS $2.44; AI semi revenue **$10.8B (+143% YoY)**, ~49% of total; AI bookings **>$30B**.
- **Q3 total guide ~$29.4B (+84% YoY)** — but the **Q3 AI-chip number (~$16B) came in below the ~$17.2B buy-side whisper**, and "priced-for-perfection" positioning punished it: **−12.6% June 4 (worst day since Jan 2025), −6.27% June 5.**
- FY26 AI revenue **reaffirmed $56B (~180% growth)**; FY27 **reiterated >$100B**. The structural story is intact; the *expectations bar* was the problem.

## Key facts
- **Q2 FY26 (reported June 3 AMC):** Revenue **$22.2B, +48% YoY**. GAAP net income $9.3B / GAAP diluted EPS $1.91; non-GAAP net income $12.1B / non-GAAP EPS **$2.44**.
- **AI semiconductor revenue $10.8B, +143% YoY** (~49% of total). AI bookings **exceeded $30B** — extended demand visibility.
- **Q3 FY26 guide:** revenue **~$29.4B (+84% YoY)**; non-GAAP operating margin ~67%; adj. EBITDA ~68% of revenue.
- **Full-year:** FY26 AI semi revenue **reaffirmed ~$56B (~180% growth)**; FY27 **reiterated >$100B**, backed by multi-year contracts + major hyperscaler orders.
- **Stock reaction:** −12.6% June 4 (steepest single-day since Jan 2025), −6.27% June 5. The trigger was the ~$16B Q3 AI-chip line vs ~$17.2B whisper — i.e., a miss against *elevated* expectations, not against guidance.

## Key claims (and how confident)
- **Business is accelerating; the stock fell on positioning, not fundamentals** — HIGH. +143% AI revenue, $30B+ bookings, +84% next-Q total guide, and reaffirmed FY targets are not the markers of a thesis break.
- **The $16B vs $17.2B "miss" is consistent with the reaffirmed $56B FY26 AI guide** — HIGH. Q1+Q2 AI = $8.4B + $10.8B = $19.2B; $56B FY implies ~$36.8B H2 → ~$16–18B/quarter. The buy-side simply modeled *above* company guidance.
- **A great print getting sold 12.6% is a late-cycle sentiment tell** — MEDIUM-HIGH. Reinforces the [[overview]] exhausted-buyer / blow-off-top watch.
