# US-Iran near 14-point MOU to end war + reopen Strait of Hormuz

## TL;DR
- US and Iran reportedly close to a **one-page, 14-point memorandum of understanding** to end the war and lift restrictions in the Strait of Hormuz.
- Terms: **Iran commits to moratorium on nuclear enrichment**; US lifts sanctions + releases frozen funds.
- Brent fell back to **~$100** on the news (vs $109 last week / $126 peak in March).
- **Trump warning**: Iran will be bombed "at a much higher level" if no deal — negotiations are fragile.

- Brent ~$100, US crude $94.81
