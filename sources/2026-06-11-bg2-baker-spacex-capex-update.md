# BG2 — SpaceX IPO, the "token-path" thesis, AI-capex update & market check (Gerstner + Baker + Fox + Tang)

> Ingested 2026-06-29 (catch-up; published 2026-06-11, two days before the SpaceX IPO). Outside the strict 24-48h sweep window but kept for net-new, falsifiable capex-vs-revenue numbers the wiki tracks.

## TL;DR
- **The capex-vs-revenue ledger, restated by the bulls:** Morgan Stanley raised its 2027 hyperscaler capex forecast from **$950B → $1.1T**; Gerstner thinks **closer to $1.5T** (and that excludes SpaceX/Crusoe-type spend). Against that, combined **AI-lab/inference revenue ~$300B for 2027** is the bear's "does the math math?" denominator. Baker says **$300B is low** — "we end *this* year well over **$200B in inference revenue**." Both call the math fine *if* you're on a path to ~$400-500B next year and **$1T+ by 2029**.
- **What "lit the fuse" in 2026:** Anthropic revenue "showed up in a major way." Gerstner: if Anthropic revenue hadn't materialized, "the whole market could be down this year." It was the overhang-clearer.
- **"Accidental profitability" + rising monetization-per-gigawatt:** the consensus going into 2026 was smooth deflationary token pricing; the opposite happened — demand outstripped supply, **monetization per gigawatt rose from ~$20B/GW (early-year) to $30-40B/GW now**. Deals made in Nov-2025 may be returning ~3x the underwritten return ("no way did they think they'd be near break-even at this part of the curve").
- **SpaceX as an AI hyperscaler / the "space compute" call option:** AI satellites pencil to **~$5B/GW to launch** vs **$20-25B/GW** for the terrestrial power/land/shell/cooling half of the BOM — a **5x cut on half the bill of materials** (terrestrial gigawatt ≈ $60B: ~$35B silicon + ~$25B land/power/cooling; space ≈ $30B/GW with lower opex). ~5MW/Starship launch. Gated on Starship V3 rapid reusability + satellite reliability.
- **xAI is the most-overlooked upside (per Gerstner):** xAI bought **Cursor** (~700-800 people, run-rating up to **~$10B revenue this year**), folding a frontier-model team into massive compute. **Grok 4.3 = 1.5T-param model now training**, with Cursor data injected into *pre-training* (not just RL). xAI reportedly secured **up to ~20% of early Vera Rubin capacity** (Tang channel checks).
- **Market check / positioning:** huge dispersion in 2026 — **semis ripped (doubles/triples)** while **Internet −16% and software −8% YTD**; SPY/NASDAQ up only via AI components. **Altimeter dialed exposure from "large" → "medium-small"** — expects a consolidation / "breather" before "much higher highs." Baker: the **"find the next bottleneck" game is over** — many names "went straight up a cliff," are "tired," need to rest.
