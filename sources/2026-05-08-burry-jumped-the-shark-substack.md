# Michael Burry — "The market has jumped the shark" + January 2027 SOXX puts disclosed

> **Ingest note (2026-05-31):** Burry's May 8 Substack post was not captured in real-time despite the wiki tracking him for the de-registration event (Nov 2025). Backfilling here because (a) it is the most concrete bear-case escalation by a named investor since Druckenmiller's "rotated away from AI" framing, and (b) Burry has now disclosed an actual options-structure short position (Jan 2027 SOXX puts) — a substantive escalation from de-registration silence to publicly betting on a ~30% semi decline within 8 months.

## TL;DR

- **Burry posted to his Substack ("Cassandra Unchained") on May 8, 2026 calling the market "jumped the shark."**
- **Concrete short position disclosed:** holds **January 2027 put options on iShares SOXX ETF** — effectively a bet on ~30% decline in semiconductor stocks within 8 months.
- **Top 10 stocks surged 784% in a year vs. 622% pre-dot-com**; Shiller PE 40.1 (highest since 2000 peak).
- **Carefully hedged language:** "not telling anyone to short these stocks"; acknowledges the rally could persist.

- **Position:** Jan 2027 SOXX put options (no notional disclosed in the leaked Substack excerpt; sizing unknown).

- **Behavioral observation:** Burry described listening to financial radio on a long drive — "nothing but AI, across every segment, every headline, every conversation. No inflation, no earnings, no geopolitics." He cites this as a 1999 final-irrational-stage signature.
- **Caveat:** Burry "openly cautioned against" shorting; acknowledged "the rally could persist in the near term even if the fundamentals do not support current valuations."
