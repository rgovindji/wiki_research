# Applied Materials Q2 FY2026 Earnings

## TL;DR

- **Beat both lines + raised CY26 guide.** Rev $7.91B (+11% YoY, record), non-GAAP EPS $2.86 (+20% YoY, vs $2.68 cons); GAAP EPS $3.51 (+33%). Q3 guide $8.95B ± $500M / non-GAAP EPS $3.36 ± $0.20.
- **CY26 semi equipment growth guide RAISED from ">20%" to ">30%."** First time AMAT has explicitly upgraded its own annual outlook this cycle.
- **DRAM = 29% of Semiconductor Systems** (vs Foundry/Logic 67%, Flash 4%) — HBM ramp embedded in the mix. AMAT separately noted DRAM as % of segment up materially YoY.

## Key facts

### Financials
| Metric | Q2 FY26 | YoY |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue | $7.91B (record) | +11% |
| GAAP gross margin | 49.9% | — |

| GAAP operating margin | 31.9% | — |
| GAAP operating income | $2.52B | — |
| GAAP EPS | $3.51 | +33% |

### Segment breakdown
- **Semiconductor Systems:** $5.97B revenue, 35.1% operating margin. Mix: **Foundry/Logic 67% · DRAM 29% · Flash 4%**.
- **Applied Global Services (AGS):** $1.67B, 29.2% operating margin. High-margin annuity.
- **Display (other):** -$56M operating loss.

### Q3 FY26 guidance
- Revenue: **$8.95B ± $500M** (mid-point implies +18% YoY)
- Non-GAAP EPS: **$3.36 ± $0.20**

### Capital return
- **15% dividend increase to $0.53/share** — 9th consecutive annual raise
- $765M returned to shareholders Q2 ($400M buybacks + $365M dividends)

### Strategic moves
- **Acquiring ASMPT's NEXX advanced packaging business** — bolts on advanced packaging capacity
- **EPIC Center partnerships announced** with TSMC, SK hynix, Micron, and universities — collaborative R&D center

## Key claims (and how confident)

- **"We now expect our semiconductor equipment business to grow more than 30 percent in calendar 2026."** — CEO Gary Dickerson. **HIGH CONFIDENCE.** This is an explicit, on-the-record guide raise from his prior ">20%" outlook. The +10pp lift is material.
- **"AI demand is now in full force."** — CFO Brice Hill. **HIGH CONFIDENCE** as company commentary.
- **AMAT positioned across "leading-edge logic, DRAM, and advanced packaging"** — the three fastest-growing AI WFE markets. **HIGH CONFIDENCE**, consistent with prior 15-of-19-HBM-process-steps framing in [[AMAT]] page.
- **"Tailwinds from faster semi business growth offset by headwinds from flattish China mix"** — CFO commentary. **CAVEAT:** China share is no longer growing but isn't collapsing either — export controls remain a structural cap on this market.
