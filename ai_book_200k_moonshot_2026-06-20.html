Kill-switches — the part that protects the downside

  • The one signal that ends the book: a hyperscaler cuts AI capex guidance (not a soft beat — an actual cut). On that print, sell the lottery sleeve and the OTM basket same day, and roll core LEAPS down to stock.
  • LEAPS discipline: never let a LEAPS go below ~6 months to expiry unrolled; never add to a losing OTM position; cap any single LEAPS line at the % shown — they grow fast and silently over-concentrate.
  • Stop framing: equities -30% from entry → reassess thesis (not auto-sell); LEAPS are pre-defined max-loss (the premium) so no stop needed, which is exactly why leverage lives in options not margin.
  • No margin, no shorting. Convexity comes only from long options here. Margin turns "lost the bet" into "owe money" — outside the mandate.
  • Tax/rolling cash stays untouched as the 5% reserve.
